About Us

We are John and Mike, travel agents specializing in the beautiful Mexican Riviera. Whether you're dreaming of a Disney vacation, planning a cruise, or seeking adventure, we're here to help make your travel dreams come true.

Together with our two dogs, Button and Elijah, we've spent years exploring and falling in love with different destinations, especially the Riviera Maya region of Mexico.

Our journey began when we sold our home and most of our possessions, keeping only what we could fit into our renovated motorhome, Yolonda. Realizing this was a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, we decided to make the most of our journey by traveling across the US before settling in Mexico.

We have lived outside of Tulum for the last few years and have continued to explore the beauty that Mexico has to offer.

At John E Travels, we offer more than just Riviera Maya experiences. Whether you're planning a magical Disney trip, a relaxing cruise, or an exciting European adventure, we have the expertise to make your travel dreams a reality. Our services include custom itineraries, accommodation arrangements, dining reservations, and special experiences to ensure your trip is seamless and memorable.

We believe in the transformative power of travel for mental health. Travel can reduce stress, enhance creativity, promote personal growth, improve mood, and encourage mindfulness. Our mission is to help you experience these benefits by providing personalized service and expert advice.

Explore our blog for travel tips and inspiration, and contact us today to start planning your next unforgettable journey. Let us help you create lasting memories with your loved ones, no matter where your dreams take you.
